door;  counter for houses;  door radical (no. 63)

Radical: 戶 (door).   Strokes: 4画.   Elements: 一尸.   Pinyin: hù.   Hangul: 호 [ho].   Nanori: え / へe / he.

Jōyō Kanji 2nd Grade.   JLPT N2.
