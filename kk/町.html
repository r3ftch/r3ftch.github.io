チョウ/CHŌ/    まち/machi/CHOU/チョウ/    machi/まち/

town;  village;  block;  street

Radical: 田 (field).   Strokes: 7画.   Elements: 一田亅.   Variants: 甼.   Pinyin: tǐng / dīng.   Hangul: 정 [jeong].

Jōyō Kanji 1st Grade.   JLPT N4.
