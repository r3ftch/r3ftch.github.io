ドウ/DŌ/    トウ/TŌ/    みち/michi/DOU/ドウ/    TOU/トウ/    michi/みち/

road-way;  street;  district;  journey;  course;  moral;  teachings

Radical: 辶 (walk).   Strokes: 12/11画.   Elements: 辶自并首.   Variants: 噵.   Pinyin: dào.   Hangul: 도 [do].   Nanori: さ / じ / ど / みつsa / ji / do / mitsu.

Jōyō Kanji 2nd Grade.   JLPT N4.
