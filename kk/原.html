ゲン/GEN/    はら/hara/GEN/ゲン/    hara/はら/

meadow;  original;  primitive;  field;  plain;  prairie;  tundra;  wilderness

Radical: 厂 (cliff).   Strokes: 10画.   Elements: 小白厂.   Variants: 厡.   Pinyin: yuán.   Hangul: 원 [weon].   Nanori: た / ばる / ら / わた / わらta / baru / ra / wata / wara.
